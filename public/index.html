About This Blog

Rifle. Rod, and Club is a place where I share the gear, tools, and experiences that fit the lifestyle I live every day. I am an eight year Navy veteran who has spent most of my life outdoors golfing, fishing, hunting, and pushing myself in the gym. This blog is where I break down the equipment I use, the things I have learned, and the items that truly make a difference. Whether it is a new club, a fishing setup, hunting gear, or workout essentials, everything here is based on real use and honest opinions. If you live the same kind of lifestyle or want to, this is the spot.
